● FBR Digital Invoicing — live integration experience

E-invoicing is no longer optional. Make it invisible instead.

Under SRO 69(I)/2025, every sales-tax registered business must issue electronic invoices through FBR's system — with the phased deadlines of SRO 1852(I)/2025 already in force. I've built and run this integration in production: real invoices, real IRNs, real audits.

✓Direct PRAL / FBR API integration — invoices submitted in real time from your own system, no manual portal entry.
✓Validate before you submit — every bill is dry-run checked against FBR rules first, so rejections happen privately, not officially.
✓All scenario types handled — standard rate, exempt, zero-rated, 3rd Schedule retail price, reduced rate and more (28 FBR scenarios).
✓IRN + QR on every invoice — FBR invoice number and verification QR printed automatically on your tax invoice layout.
✓HS-code intelligence — search FBR's 13,000+ item catalog by plain words, with valid unit-of-measure enforced per code.
✓Real-time reporting — see submitted, validated, pending and failed invoices at a glance; nothing slips to month-end.
Already past deadline? The integration mandate is enforced in phases since late 2025. If you're registered for sales tax and still invoicing manually, every day adds exposure. Retrofitting compliance into your existing flow is exactly the kind of work I do.

Frequently asked questions

How much does custom software cost? +
It depends on scope, so I work in phases: a first phase that solves your most expensive problem (often invoicing + inventory), priced and scheduled in writing before we start. You see working software early and decide each next phase with real information — no six-figure leap of faith.
Can you integrate with software we already use? +
Usually, yes. I've integrated with government APIs (FBR/PRAL), Excel-based workflows, PDF document pipelines and third-party services. If your current tool has an API, an export, or even just a consistent file format, it can almost certainly be connected.
Is FBR Digital Invoicing supported? +
Yes — this is a specialty, not a checkbox. I've built a production FBR Digital Invoicing integration: real-time submission via PRAL, pre-validation before submission, IRN and QR on printed invoices, scenario handling (standard, exempt, zero-rated, 3rd Schedule and more) and a compliance dashboard. Sandbox testing happens before any real invoice is sent.
Do you provide maintenance and support? +
Yes. Every project includes a support window, and long-term I offer ongoing maintenance: monitoring, fixes, small improvements and priority help. Most clients keep me on as their de-facto software department.
How long does development take? +
A focused first phase typically ships in 4–8 weeks. You'll see clickable progress weekly from the start, and because delivery is phased, your team starts benefiting long before the "full vision" is complete.
Can modules be added later? +
That's the whole design philosophy. The foundation (users, permissions, companies, items, clients) is built once; accounting, POS, HR or analytics snap onto it later without rewrites. Start small, grow when ready.
