About Hakimi Traders

Hakimi Traders is a leading supplier of pneumatic fittings, equipment, and general industrial supplies in Pakistan. With over 15 years of experience, we serve some of the largest industrial groups in the country.

We specialize in providing high-quality pneumatic components from world-renowned brands, along with expert repair and servicing of pneumatic equipment including cylinders, FRL units, and solenoid valves.
